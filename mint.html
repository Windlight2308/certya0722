企鹅袁敏熙 & 北极熊邓允晴

（ London, UK ）

It's a clear day today.

Penguin: Excuse me, I am a penguin from the South Pole and I want to take a trip to the Arctic Pole. I got lost. Could you tell me how I can get there?

Polar bear: Nice to meet you, sir! I am from the Arctic Pole and this is London! The Arctic Pole is very far from here. I think you can buy a guidebook about it first.

Penguin: Great! Could you tell me where is the nearest bookshop?

Polar bear: Certainly. Go straight down the Bristol Road and turn left at the first crossing. Then go two blocks along the street. There is a big bookshop on the right corner of the third block.

Penguin: Thanks a lot.

( half an hour later )

Penguin: Now I get a guidebook. Would you like to travel to the Arctic Pole with me?

Polar bear: Sorry, I want to travel to the South Pole.

Penguin: Oh, you had better buy some warm clothes.

Polar bear: What a good idea! But where is the shopping centre?

Penguin: Don't you know?

Polar bear: I am not from around here.

Penguin: Let's ask the policeman over there!

-The End-
